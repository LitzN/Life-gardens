Our Services

Design

Our designers have experience with gardens of all sizes and styles from rooftop gardens to country gardens. They will take you through popular styles and work with you to tailor these to your taste.

Build

Our highly skilled and rated building team have provided customers with landscaping, creating ponds, water features & gazebos. Guaranteed to make your dream garden a reality.

Plant

Our plant suppliers are able to offer you a breathtaking selection of flowers, shrubs and trees at discount prices.

Maintenance

Our knowledgeable gardeners can help you maintain a healthy thriving garden and create displays of annual plants for a fresh look so you can enjoy your garden throughout the seasons.

Our Process

Step 1

Fill out our online form or speak to an advisor. We use the information to match the perfect designer for your project.

Step 2

Your designer will contact you the next working day and organise a viewing of your garden. They will discuss different design options with you.

Step 3

Your designer will then produce plans based on your requirements and also provide a quote for the work. Once you're happy with the plans your builder or gardener can start work!

Why choose us?

We believe in creating beautiful outdoor living spaces. With our service you will be guided through the whole process giving you the ability to customise your package to create your perfect garden and keep prices low. Our highly skilled team are passionate about transforming spaces and leaving customers with beautiful gardens that withstand the test of time.
